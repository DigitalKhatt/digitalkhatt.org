About

DigitalKhatt is an advanced Arabic typesetter based on a Metafont-designed parametric font that can generate a glyph with a given width during layout and justification while respecting the curvilinear nature of Arabic letters. The typesetter extends the OpenType layout engine to support the varying width and shape of the glyphs.

This site presents the Holy Quran typeset using DigitalKhatt to be used as an ebook application. Also, since Quranic transcriptions are the gold standards for Arabic script, we will be able to measure the quality of the typesetter by comparing it to the Medina Mushaf, from which the glyphs have been digitalized, in order to guide the future developments until reaching a high-quality digital copy comparable to the handwritten one.
